美国头部播客主持人，《[[shows/call-her-daddy|Call Her Daddy]]》创办人与主播；与 [[creators/joe-rogan|Joe Rogan]] 同为名人级播客明星的代表，"播客从草根麦克风转向名人主导"拐点的标志人物。详见 [[markets/us|美国市场]]。

## 职业轨迹

- **2018** 与 Sofia Franklyn 在 Barstool Sports 旗下创办《Call Her Daddy》。2022-How corporate takeovers are fundamentally changing podcasting _ Nieman Journalism Lab
- **2020.05** 与 Barstool 爆发公开合约纠纷，成为"平台与创作者权益张力"的行业案例。2020-从疫情到 Spotify，这份 2020 播客 “半年报” 请收好
- **2021** Spotify 以约 $60M 签下节目独家。2022-How corporate takeovers are fundamentally changing podcasting _ Nieman Journalism Lab
- **2024** 接近与 [[roles/siriusxm|SiriusXM]] 达成近 **$100M** 协议（含最低收听目标条款）；独家协议到期后节目回归 YouTube。交易细节见 [[shows/call-her-daddy|节目页]]。2024-A Few Blockbuster Podcasts Are Making All the Money 2025-视频播客
