美国科技记者，也是《Longform》播客的联合主持人之一。2024 年发起独立播客[[shows/shell-game|《Shell Game》]]，被视为美国[[niches/narrative-podcast|叙事播客]]后崩塌时代的范例。

## 关键观察

> "It feels like podcasts speed-ran the development of an industry to the decline of an industry. It went from people were making absolutely no money, to people were making fortunes big enough where they'd never have to work again, to 'There's not a budget for that' in less than 10 years."

## 操作策略

- 拒绝被金主推向"AI 的分析性结论"，而是把节目做得"as weird as possible"
- 小团队：自己 + 制作人 Sophie Bridges + 妻子 Samantha Henig（NYT 音频部门创办人）
- 收入：Substack 订阅（几乎全部）→ Independent Media Initiative $50K 奖 → S2 与 Kaleidoscope 合作
