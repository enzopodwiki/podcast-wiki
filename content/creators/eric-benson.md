美国记者、播客主持人、制作人。曾任 Texas Monthly staff writer，2020.12 离职全职做播客。2025 年为 Rolling Stone 撰写《Narrative Podcasts Are Disappearing. What Happened?》——本来源文章——以亲历者+采访者双重身份记录了美国[[niches/narrative-podcast|叙事播客]]行业从兴起到崩塌的全过程。

## 个人叙事弧

- 2017 春：朋友 Matthew Shaer 邀他做播客《Over My Dead Body》，"我以为自己已经太晚了"
- 2019：上线即榜首，影视化
- 2020.12：辞 Texas Monthly 全职做播客；"I had caught the bug. I wasn't the only one."
- 2023-2025：连续两个节目被委托方砍掉
- 2025 初：自 2018 年以来第一次没有新节目 offer
- 2025 年夏：开始报道《Narrative Podcasts Are Disappearing》（即本来源）；写作期间 Pineapple Street 宣布停业，他曾为该工作室主持/撰写两档节目

## 行业视角

> "An entire generation of journalists who started in podcasting are leaving and are unlikely to return."

> "Whatever comes next will be built up in the shadow of that history — that it all looked so good and that so many of us bought into the mirage."
